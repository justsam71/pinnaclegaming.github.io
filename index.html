About Us:

Our company has been created in 2021 and since then we have been trying our best to be number one at what we do. So far we may be don’t have as much experience but trust us when we say that others can only be jealous of our enthusiasm!

So far the company consists only of one poor solo developer who is working every day and night but we are looking for more people with the same enthusiasm and the will to work for free.

If you, by any chance, enjoyed playing our games, please, don’t hesitate to leave the feedback. And if you feel generous enough, you can always support our company by a small donation. But don’t worry, you don’t have to do that. All of our games are free and we appreciate every each of you. Thank you!
